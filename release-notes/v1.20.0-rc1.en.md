**Beta (pre-release) for testers — please try it and send feedback.**

This build adds a big new feature: a **Cut / Split tool** to trim audio and video files — for example to remove the commercials from a recorded show, or to split a file into pieces. It is built for a full keyboard and screen-reader workflow.

- **New "Cut / Split…" tool** — right-click an audio or video file in the list and choose "Cut / Split…". It opens a dedicated editor window with its own menu bar.
- **Mark what to remove or keep** — mark a region to discard (for ads), add cut points to split into pieces, and toggle any segment between keep and discard.
- **Listen while you edit** — play the file, pause and resume, or turn on "Montage mode" to hear only the result (the discarded parts are skipped), starting from wherever you are. "Verify the cut" plays exactly how a cut will sound in the exported file. Optional audio "scrub" plays a short preview as you move.
- **Find ad breaks by ear** — jump to the previous/next silence in the file.
- **Fine navigation** — move by a chosen step (10 ms to 1 minute), jump between cut points, go to an exact time, and (for videos with several audio tracks) switch the audio track you preview.
- **Export your way** — export one re-joined file with the discarded parts removed, or one file per kept part. You choose the output format and quality at export time, and the editor stays open so you can export again or keep adjusting.
- **Undo / redo**, **save and reopen a cut project**, and a confirmation before closing so you don't lose your work.

Everything is announced with your screen reader, and every action is available both from the menus and with a keyboard shortcut (press F1 in the editor for the full list).
