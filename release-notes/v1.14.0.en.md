- **MPEG-TS file support (.ts, .m2ts, .mts)**: you can now add files from TV recordings or AVCHD camcorders (`.ts`, `.m2ts`, `.mts`) and convert them like any other video — to MP4 or MKV, or to extract their audio. The app automatically sorts them into the correct tab and repairs the missing timestamps common in broadcast streams, for reliable conversion and merging.
