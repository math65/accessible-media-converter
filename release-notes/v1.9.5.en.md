- New "Preserve original metadata" option in Preferences (off by default): when enabled, conversions and merges keep the source file's tags and chapters, and audio conversions to MP3, AAC/M4A, ALAC or FLAC also keep the embedded cover art.
- Fixed converting a file to its own format in the same folder (for example M4A to M4A, MP3 to MP3 or PNG to PNG): instead of being silently skipped or failing, it now reliably writes a safe copy such as "song (1).m4a" and never touches the original.
- Update installer now runs with a simple progress bar instead of the click-through setup wizard — applying an update no longer requires clicking Next, Next, Next.
- Updated the embedded FFmpeg build.
